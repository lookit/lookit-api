Contact

Technical difficulties

To report any technical difficulties during participation, please contact our team by email at lookit-tech@mit.edu.

Questions about the studies

If you have any questions or concerns about the studies, please check our FAQ. You can also contact the researchers running a particular study (their contact info is listed on the study page) or the Lookit team at lookit@mit.edu.

For researchers

Lookit is available to all developmental researchers, and the code is entirely open-source. We would love for you to join our community! To learn more about running your own studies or contributing to the project, please check out the guide to getting started.

Several initial test studies of a prototype Lookit platform were completed in 2015; you can read about the results, and strengths and limitations of the platform, in these papers.
